How It Works

From Zero to Live
in Less Than a Week

01 / DISCOVERY
We Talk on WhatsApp

You tell me about your business — your services, your customers, what you want the site to do. I ask 7 simple questions. No forms, no meetings needed.

02 / BUILD
I Design & Code

I build your site from scratch in VS Code — custom design, your brand colours, your content. Not a drag and drop template. Clean hand-written code.

03 / REVIEW
You Check Everything

I send you a live preview link. You review every detail and request any changes. Nothing goes live until you're completely happy.

04 / LAUNCH
Your Site Goes Live

We connect your .co.ke domain and deploy to Cloudflare. Your site is live, fast, and secure with a free SSL certificate. Customers can find you from day one.
