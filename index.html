Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a website take to build? ↓

Most projects are done in 3–5 days from when you send me your business details. Rush projects are available for an additional fee.

Do I need to provide anything? ↓

Just your business name, services, location, phone number, and any photos you have. I handle the writing, design, and coding.

What about my domain name? ↓

I recommend a .co.ke domain — it's cheap (around KES 1,000–1,300/year) and trusted by Kenyan customers. You can register it on Safaricom or Truehost. I'll guide you through it step by step.

Are there monthly fees after launch? ↓

No monthly fees from me. The only recurring cost is your domain renewal (~KES 1,300/year) and hosting if applicable. That's it.

What if I need changes after my site launches? ↓

The first 30 days include free support for minor fixes. After that, edits are charged at a small flat rate depending on what's needed — no retainer or subscription required.

Will my site work on mobile phones? ↓

Yes — 100%. Every site I build is fully mobile-responsive. It will look great and load fast on any phone, tablet, or computer.

How do we stay in touch during the project? ↓

Entirely on WhatsApp. I send you updates, preview links, and check-ins throughout. Most clients find it very easy to manage.
