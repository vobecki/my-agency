How It Works

Zero to Live in 5 Days

No technical knowledge needed on your side. You send me your details — I handle everything else.

01 · DISCOVERY
We Talk on WhatsApp

You tell me about your business, your customers, and what you want the site to do. I ask 7 simple questions. No forms, no meetings, no wasted time.

02 · BUILD
I Design & Code

I build your site from scratch in VS Code — custom design, your colours, your content. Hand-written HTML and CSS. Not a drag-and-drop builder output.

03 · REVIEW
You Check Everything

I send you a live preview link. You review every page, every word, every button. Request changes. Nothing goes live until you're fully satisfied.

04 · LAUNCH
Your Site Goes Live

We connect your .co.ke domain on Safaricom and deploy to Cloudflare. Free SSL certificate. Fast globally. Customers find you from day one.
